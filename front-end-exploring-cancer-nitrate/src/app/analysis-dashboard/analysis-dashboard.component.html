Nitrate, Water, and Cancer

Explore the relationship yourself.

This app allows anyone to explore the relationship between cancer rates and nitrate concentration in Wisconsin water.

Introduction

The question we want to explore is: What is the relationship between Cancer Rate and Nitrate Concentration in Water?

  • Independent (Explanatory) Variable: Nitrate Concentration in Water
  • Dependent Variable: Cancer Rate

Below are results of linear regression where we try to use nitrate concentration values to predict cancer rates in Wisconsin census tracts. The map shows cancer rate residuals (differences between predicted cancer rate and actual cancer rate), while the text box shows full regression diagnostics which can be used to determine statistical significance and prediction quality.
